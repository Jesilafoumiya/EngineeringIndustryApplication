ABOUT US

WE DON'T JUST BUILD PRODUCTS, WE BUILD TRUST.

“Anpam Engineering“, is the fastest growing Manufacturer, and Supplier firm of innovative Noise Control Equipment which was established in the year 2000.

We are backed with modernized R & D facilities to bring forth wide assortments of high performing Vent Silencer, Sound Proof Cabin and Exhaust Silencers. Our product line is a thoughtful mix of design, planning and performance which makes us the preferred entity offering unmatched quality products with comprehensive benefits of study construction, international standards design, optimum functionality, easy installation, customized solutions and competitive price range.

Maheshwara

CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Maheshwara, our CEO and founder of M/s. ANPAM ENGINEERING, is credited with being one of the pioneers of the Silencer manufacturing industry in India. Under his guidance ANPAM ENGINEERING has continued to ride the waves of the challenges in the silencer manufacture for over 20 years and remain at the forefront of every technological revolution since 2000.

He steered ANPAM ENGINEERING to add up several valuable satisfied customers. His pioneering spirit and belief in engineering and manufacturing shaped the course of the organization and is the reason that today ANPAM ENGINEERING , one of the largest silencer manufacturer and Exporter unit in India.

 

Our driving force “Mr. A.P. Maheshwara“, has experience in the field of evolving innovative techniques for noise control systems. His vast industrial knowledge motivates us to successfully nurture the leading position in the industry.
